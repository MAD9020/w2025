# Week 14: Final Project Work session and 1:1 feedback

Learning Objective

By the end of this week, students will:

  • Have an opportunity to get 1:1 feedback from instructors on their work

Homework: Figma Design & Prototype Review :::

  • Students should have their final project designed end-to-end.

  • There can be some details missing, but students should be prepared to share with instructor:

    • the key screens visual designs on the selected platform
    • a navigation model figured out
    • as much as possible ready for final presentation to get helpful feedback from instructor
