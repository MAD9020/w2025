# Week 5 - Components

# Step 1: Paper prototype & component audit

Start by identifying what would be a required component for your midterm project. A component is an element that repeats in your interface and is scalable and reusable with varied data. Take a photo of your paper prototype and add it to your Figma file in a page called "components". Add a list of components you need if you are not able to create all of them.

# Step 2: Follow the tutorial below to create a card component



# In your design system component page:

  • Start with a proper grid on a mobile frame to measure the right size of your card followig the video.
  • Make sure you follow enhancement details and image aspect ratio for card components.
  • Once your card component is ready, publish your library to update the new components

# In your midterm file

  • Use a mobile frame with a grid style that your previously created.
  • learn nested autolayout for creating rows of card items.
  • Learn how to make you card items scrollable in a prototype of the page.
  • Create cart and Badge and apply variables to prototype add to cart.

# Step 3: Apply the knowledge & customize your card for your Midterm

With all the previous learning, customize your card component. The card should contain at least contain the following:

  • Movie Image: Ensure a 4:3 ratio
  • Movie Title
  • Movie description
  • Movie rating
  • Call To Action Button (CTA)
