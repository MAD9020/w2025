# Week 1 - User Needs and Business Goals

# Details

Select an application from the list below or preferably an application that you personally use on regular basis. The application must have a desktop and mobile version. The application criteria is to allow you to perform complex tasks like banking or transport. Identify the following:

# Business Goals:

  • At least 3 primary business goals of the full website
  • At least 3 primary business goals of the mobile site/app

# User Needs

  • At least 3 primary user goals of the mobile site/app
  • At least 2 primary user goals of the mobile site/app

# List of products/services/sites to choose from

1 to 6 6 to 10 13 to 18
1. Algonquin College 7. Globe and Mail 13. Ottawa Public Library
2. Home Depot 8. Ottawa.com 14. TSN
3. Dropbox 9. McDonald's 15. Skip the Dishes
4. The Weather Network 10. Instagram 16. Facebook
5. Google Maps 11. Google Drive 17. Allrecipes
6. OC Transpo 12. Cineplex 18. Service Canada

# Deliverable for this exercise:

  • (optional) Create a Mad9020 folder in your Figma workspace
  • Create a new figma file and give it the assignment name
  • Add a cover page or ensure your name is in the file
  • Add your discoveries to the frame
  • Add a screenshots of the application you investigated, ensure to hide any sensitive personal data or details
  • Design Bonus: Make you findings look good.

Note

  • When thinking of Business goals think like a business, what are they trying to acheive?
  • When thinking of User Needs, put yourself in the user’s shoes. "As a user, I want to ..."

# Submission

  • Export your frame to a PNG and submit it to the assignement in Brightspace
  • Add a figma link to your file, always make sure when you click share that the link is public with edit access to anyone who has the link.
  • Open Brightspace LMS and go to the Activities > Assignments page.
  • Go to the Week 1 - User goals and business needs assignment.
  • Upolad your png on the assignment page and click the submit button.
